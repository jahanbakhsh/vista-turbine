Welcome to Vista Turbine

VISTA’s dedicated engine shop performs all levels of on- and off-wing aircraft engine work in wide range of maintenance activities from borescope inspections to full engine overhaul. VISTA obtained the PART-145 certification for a maintenance organization from the Civil Aviation Organisation of Iran. We offer top quality services to our customers. Our newly engine shop is staffed by highly skilled engine technicians and engineers who provide major repairs on Pratt & Whitney, CFM International and IAE engines including full major periodic inspection (MPI). We also perform line maintenance found on the aircraft we maintain. VISTA ensures that our customer’s engines have been serviced according to OEM manuals and recommendations.
